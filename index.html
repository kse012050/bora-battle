Change to the Benefits System of COMPETZ NFTs and Refund Related Information
We’d like to sincerely apologize to those who have been supporting our COMPETZ platform.
During our examination of the system which COMPETZ PFPs and Solitaire Skin NFTs (hereby referred to as COMPETZ NFTs) revolve around, we eventually reached the conclusion that we have no other choice but to reform our NFT benefits if we want to ensure that we provide a sustainable and stable service.
We admit that this change to the benefits system for COMPETZ is significant, and may come as a disappointment to some in our community.
Listed below are the new COMPETZ NFT benefits as well as refund-related information. Please read through the details to help you determine if you would like to keep or refund your COMPETZ NFT(s).

Benefits Linked to Gameplay:
- Benefits will now take effect as one progress through the game(s).

Holder Benefits:
(1) Lost GEM Regeneration
• When holders play games on the COMPETZ platform and lose GEMs as a result of defeat, a portion of the holders’ losses is regenerated.
• The proportion of the lost GEMs regenerated is determined by the rarity of that holder’s COMPETZ NFT.
• If a holder possesses multiple COMPETZ NFTs, the one with the greatest level of rarity is applied.
• These lost GEMs may be freely recovered the next corresponding month.
(2) Conversion Fee Discount
• When converting GEMs into PTZ, holders will receive a discount on the transaction fees.
• The proportion of the fees discounted will be determined by the rarity of that holder’s COMPETZ NFT.
• If a holder possesses multiple COMPETZ NFTs, the discount rate for the highest level of rarity is applied.
* not applicable for Solitaire Skin NFTs
(3) License for Commercial Use
Holders will be entitled to use the image of the COMPETZ NFTs for a variety of commercial uses, including the right to manufacture and sell T-shirts, mugs, key chains, etc.
Refund Information:
Holders may also opt for a refund for the COMPETZ NFTs. Holders will be refunded based on the following criteria:

1) Refund Amount
The refund amount will be the greater of: (a) the amount paid by the final Holder for the COMPETZ NFTs (“Purchase Price”), and (b) the one-week prior average market price at which the COMPETZ NFTs of the same rarity level was traded (“Average Market Price”), calculated as of the time of this announcement (June 4, 2022 at 20:50pm KST (UTC+9)). For any COMPETZ NFTs with a rarity level fewer than five (5) market transaction histories, the refund amount will be assessed by taking in consideration the price trends of the COMPETZ NFTs of other rarity levels.
