GEM & BATTLE

GEM is the currency used in BORABATTLE as an entry fee, and can be earned by winning a battle. GEM can be swapped to BORA at the BORA portal and vice versa.

BORABATTLE NFT

BORABATTLE NFT is designed based on pop art style with panda motifs. The NFT can be used as a PFP within BORABATTLE to show off its unique personality to other players. The NFT is divided into four grades, Legendary - Epic - Rare - Common.

NFT HOLDER BENEFITS

The richer premium benefit will be provided with the higher the NFT grade, and the premium benefits will overlap as acquiring more than one NFT. Premium benefits will be determined later.
